Bringing Old Photos Back to Life

Welcome to Photo-Restore-Pro, where cherished memories are restored with care and precision. As a dedicated photo restorer, I specialize in repairing, enhancing, and colorizing old photographs—breathing new life into treasured images.

About Me

My name is Patrick, and I am a passionate and highly skilled photo restoration expert with over a decade of experience. Working from home, I have dedicated myself to the meticulous craft of restoring damaged, faded, and timeworn photographs to their original beauty.

My expertise ensures that every image retains its authenticity while regaining the vibrancy and detail it once had.Despite personal challenges due to my disability, I've built a career that allows me to apply my talent in a meaningful and fulfilling way.

Every photograph I restore represents a cherished memory. With an unwavering attention to detail and a deep appreciation for history, I breathe new life into treasured moments — preserving them for generations to come.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our photo restoration services.

All photo restorations are completed within 1 minute using advanced AI technology. No matter the damage, we deliver fast, high-quality results without the wait.

We can restore photos with various types of damage including fading, discoloration, scratches, tears, water damage, mold spots, creases, and more. Even severely damaged photos can often be restored to a remarkable degree.

We strongly recommend sending digital copies (scans or high-quality photos) of your originals to avoid any risk of damage or loss during shipping. However, if you prefer to send original photos, we accept them at your own risk and will return them with your restored copies. We handle all originals with the utmost care.

Yes, we specialize in high-quality photo colorization. Our process combines AI technology with manual artistry to create realistic, historically accurate colors. We can work with your guidance on specific color preferences or conduct research to ensure period-appropriate coloring. Colorization is included in our Advanced and Premium restoration packages.

We accept most common image formats including JPG, PNG, TIFF, and BMP. For best results, we recommend high-resolution TIFF files if possible. We deliver restored photos in high-quality JPG or PNG formats by default, but can provide TIFF or other formats upon request. All restored photos are delivered in high resolution suitable for printing.
